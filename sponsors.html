JKS Jewellers

Christened as JK Silver and Co, JK Prasad ventured into Silver Jewels and articles business in Kolkata in the year 1989. Caring the best of Silver articles and jewels to big and small retailer paved a way to set up a manufacturing plant. Later the company ventured into Kolkata handcrafted gold ornaments in the year 1996 in Bangalore and in the year 2004 JKS JEWELS PVT LTD was launched. The Organisation is engaged in offering an entire variety of Kolkata Handmade Jewels, Temple Jewels, Antique Jewels, Derewala Italian Jewels, Hollow and Cartier Bracelet and Chains.

They are one of the chief suppliers of the international companies Walmart and Nautica. It's running for three decades now and has been at the top of the charts for seven years now.

Mayur Uniquoters Ltd.

Being the most preferred supplier of artificial leather is what was at the genesis of the formation of Mayur Uniquoters Ltd. Mayur has shown phenomenal growth so much so, that in 2012, we made way into the 'Forbes Asia Top 200 under $1Bn enterprises' in the Asia-Pacific region. We are the largest manufacturer of artificial leather, using the 'Release Paper Transfer Coating Technology' in India. The guiding principle of the organization has always been fulfillment of customer requirement and progressive thinking which is now the culture of the organization.
