Suresh Raina

One of India’s finest athletic talents – Mr. Suresh Raina has dominated the cricket pitch. His international debut came at the age of 19, and since then, he’s acquired many records and distinctions to his name. In 2011, he played an integral role in team India’s victorious conquest of the World Cup. Mr. Raina’s IPL achievements include having the most runs scored in the tournament, and being a two-time league champion, with the Chennai Super kings. His off-the- field endeavors include starting the Gracia Raina foundation, which works closely with underprivileged mothers and children, in dealing with health issues. We are in anticipation for the unlocking, unboxing, and unleashing of Mr. Raina’s ideas and stories, come December 1 st .

Zeeny Jhelumi

Author, storyteller, and educator, Ms. Zeeny Jhelumi is someone who aims to make learning fun. She served as an editor of Mkids – a children’s publishing house, in Delhi. Over the years she has written countless stories and poems for children. She often works with them, in her learning centre, called The Storytellers. Ms. Jhelumi’s works are a prominent feature of a multitude Book Fairs, including those held in Paris, and Frankfurt. She also conducts workshops on story-telling, in both England and India. Hailing from the Trinity Guildhall School of Music and Drama, in London, Ms. Jhelumi won the Best editor award, at her school. On March of this year, she was honored at the London-Asian Film Festival, for her work as a writer. Hoping to unlock, unbox, and unleash the imaginative corners of our minds, we excitedly await the arrival of Ms Zeeny Jhelumi at TEDxYouth@JPIS, 2017.

Arjun Puri

Head of Admissions and Outreach for the O. P. Jindal Global University - Mr. Arjun Puri has received education from the most prestigious learning platforms like La Martiniere for Boys in Calcutta and graduated from the University of St Andrews in 2007. Sir, then worked as a banker with Merrill Lynch International Bank for close to 5 years. There are moments in life where we are asked “what do you want to be when you grow up?” and like every child, Mr. Puri was asked the same. The moment he felt torn between his profession and passion, he started to do some thinking. He is an example of an idealist, where he doesn't only inspire a thousand others to walk on the path of passion but also demonstrates his career as a perfect balance between success and satisfaction. After moving back to India, Mr. Puri joined ITC’s rural development and farmer empowerment programme, E-Choupal. During these sudden changes what kept him going was his mantra, “I had chosen to undertake this journey and I will see it through.” His desire to give back to the society took his next career stop back to the education and career counseling sector. Currently, sir works as the Head of Admissions and Outreach for the O. P. Jindal Global University. We eagerly await Mr. Puri’s passion, commitment, and compassion to ignite a fire of curiosity and zeal in our minds.
